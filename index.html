I am Zuhyre Shehjad Kamal Chowdhury, a Class 8 student from Bangladesh with a deep, disciplined passion for mathematics, computer science, and competitive programming.

My journey began with a genuine fascination for problem-solving — the kind that goes beyond textbooks. I actively participate in the Bangladesh Mathematical Olympiad and am preparing for national-level programming competitions including the National High School Programming Contest (NHSPC).

Beyond competition, I am building toward a long-term vision that merges agricultural innovation with scalable technology — a startup idea that reflects both my roots and my ambitions. Every skill I build now is deliberate preparation for a larger purpose.

I am not preparing for exams. I am preparing for impact.
